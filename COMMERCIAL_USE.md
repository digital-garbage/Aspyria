# Commercial Use Notes

Aspyria is source-available, but it is not a free-for-all commercial product. You can play with the code, learn from it, fork it for free, and share free modified builds. You cannot sell the game, sell the code, or sell derivative software based on it.

Allowed:

- Free forks and free modified builds.
- Private modifications with no obligation to publish them.
- Monetized videos, livestreams, posts, articles, podcasts, music, tutorials, and reviews featuring Aspyria.
- Paid services that let people play or modify Aspyria, as long as the game/code/build itself is not being sold as the product.
- Free mod packs and free patches.

Not allowed without written permission:

- Selling Aspyria as a game.
- Selling the original source code.
- Selling modified Aspyria builds.
- Selling ports or derivative software based on Aspyria code.
- Bundling Aspyria into a paid software package, paid launcher, paid marketplace item, or paid game collection.

Short version: making content around Aspyria is fine. Charging for access to a service around playing or modifying it is fine. Selling Aspyria itself, its code, or derivative software made from its code is not fine.
